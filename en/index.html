Frequently Asked Questions

The topics our patients ask about most often

Is implant treatment painful?

Implant procedures are performed under local anesthesia, so pain during the procedure is not expected. Mild tenderness afterward can usually be managed comfortably.

Does professional whitening damage the teeth?

When whitening is planned under professional supervision with the right materials and after evaluating the gums and enamel, it is designed to be safe for the teeth.

Is implant treatment suitable for everyone?

Bone support, gum health, and general health status are reviewed before implant treatment. Many patients may be suitable after individualized planning.

Why is dental cleaning important?

Tartar buildup can contribute to gum disease, bad breath, and gum recession. Regular cleaning supports healthier gums and easier home care.

Is Archident Dental Clinic available 24/7?

Archident Dental Clinic offers 24/7 phone and WhatsApp access for urgent dental pain, trauma, swelling, bleeding, and unexpected dental concerns. Guidance and appointment direction are planned according to the urgency of the complaint.

How can I leave a Google review?

Patients can visit the clinic's Google Business profile through the links on the website and share their real treatment experience there.

When should I contact a dentist for an emergency toothache?

If the pain is severe, lasts longer than two days, disrupts sleep or daily life, or is accompanied by swelling, fever, bleeding, trauma, or a bad taste, prompt contact is recommended.

Can a dental abscess go away on its own?

A dental abscess can be a serious infection-related condition and should not be expected to resolve without evaluation. Swelling, throbbing pain, fever, or jaw sensitivity should be assessed by a dentist.

When is root canal treatment recommended?

Root canal treatment is commonly considered when the nerve tissue is affected by deep decay, trauma, infection, or persistent pain and the goal is to preserve the tooth.

Is sensitivity normal after whitening?

Temporary sensitivity can occur after professional whitening. This is why enamel condition, gum health, and existing restorations are checked before treatment.

Who may be suitable for zirconium crowns?

They may be considered for patients with aesthetic concerns, structural tooth loss, or a desire for natural-looking restorations after examination of gums and bite.

Why is pediatric dentistry important?

Regular dental visits in childhood help detect cavities early, support oral hygiene habits, and monitor the healthy eruption of permanent teeth.

Is orthodontic treatment only cosmetic?

No. Orthodontic treatment may also improve bite balance, chewing function, and oral hygiene access in addition to appearance.

How is a treatment plan created during the first consultation?

The first consultation reviews the complaint, oral condition, radiographs when needed, and the patient's expectations. Treatment options and priorities are then explained in a personalized way.

Can I get directions to the clinic?

Yes. You can use the Google Maps link in the contact section to get direct directions and review the location before your appointment.

Can I contact the clinic 24/7 for urgent dental complaints?

Yes. In cases such as severe pain, swelling, trauma, bleeding, or sudden dental problems, you can contact the clinic by phone or WhatsApp for quick initial guidance.

Does a painful root canal treated tooth always need to be extracted?

No. Pain in a tooth that has already had root canal treatment does not always mean extraction is required. The tooth should first be evaluated for infection, cracks, root-end problems, bite overload, or inadequate previous canal filling. In suitable cases, the tooth may be preserved with retreatment or additional restorative care.

What is endodontic retreatment?

Endodontic retreatment means reopening a tooth that has previously received root canal treatment, cleaning and disinfecting the canals again, and refilling them. It may be considered when pain, infection, or a root-end lesion persists after the earlier treatment.
